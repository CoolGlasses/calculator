Helpful Tips

  • Pressing the "c" key will clear the calculator.
  • This Calculator follows the rules regarding Order of Operations.
  • Pressing " = " will allow you to see the result, until then the equation will be dispalyed.
  • Press the Root button after a the number you want to run the operation for.
  • Hit/Clicked the wrong button? Press Backspace to delete the last entry!
